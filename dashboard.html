💡 Tips you might miss
  • Click any sidebar item to jump between Nests, Workspaces, Closed, Reminders, Stats, and Settings.
  • Hover any nest card — 📤 and ✕ buttons appear so you can ungroup or close that one nest only.
  • Hover any tab — small 📋 button lets you add a private note to that URL.
  • "Pause auto-grouping" in Settings stops TabNest from grouping new tabs until you turn it back on.
  • Undo button on toasts — after closing tabs, you have 6 seconds to bring them back.
  • Custom nests live in Settings → Your Custom Nests — make a category for anything (e.g. "Side project").
🆘 Common questions
TabNest is auto-grouping tabs I don't want grouped

Settings → Behaviour → toggle "⏸️ Pause auto-grouping". You can still re-nest manually with the toolbar button.

The colored chips in my bookmark bar — are those TabNest?

Those are Chrome's "saved tab groups" — a Chrome feature, not TabNest. Right-click each → Delete group, or disable at chrome://settings/tabs.

I closed important tabs by mistake

Toasts have an "Undo" button for 6 seconds. After that, find them in the "Closed" sidebar section — kept for 30 days.

How do I move my Pro to a different email?

Settings → TabNest Pro → "Switch account". Enter your new email — your trial or Pro state transfers, no reset.
